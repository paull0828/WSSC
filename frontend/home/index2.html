Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a seat?

+

Simply click on the "Book Your Seat" button and follow the steps to reserve your spot. You can choose your preferred date, time slot, and seating area.

What are the study space's operating hours?

+

We are open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Mon-Sat, excluding government holidays. This ensures you can study according to your own schedule.

Can I change my subscription plan later?

+

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings. Changes will take effect from the next billing cycle.

Is there WiFi available?

+

Yes, we provide high-speed WiFi to all our members. The network is secure and optimized for study and research purposes.
